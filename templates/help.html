Help

Welcome to the help page. We will try to answer your questions.

What do I do in this game?

You try to bring your opponent to 0 HP to knock them out. You do this by punching and blocking with phones in your hands.

How should I hold my phone?

In your palm, landscape mode and with the screen facing your way:

What kind of actions can I perform?

Each phone can be in three different states: block , neutral and attack. In order to block attacks both hands have to be in block mode.

How do I perform actions?

Block attacks by raising your hand in front of your face. Attack by extending your hand and rotating it palm down.
